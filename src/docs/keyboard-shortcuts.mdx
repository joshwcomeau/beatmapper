Beatmapper makes heavy use of keyboard shortcuts, especially for quality-of-life features. You can make maps without learning any of them, but your life will be much easier if you take the time to familiarize yourself with them :)

## General shortcuts

These shortcuts can be used either in the "Notes" view or the "Events" view.
